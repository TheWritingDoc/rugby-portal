## Competition Structure

### League Format
- **Round 1:** Commences in 2nd term (Home games first)
- **Round 2:** Commences in 3rd term (Away games)
- **Wednesdays:** League matches
- **Fridays:** Zonal cluster team trials (3 consecutive weeks)

### Progression
1. **Zone Winners:** Each zone determines a winner through league play
2. **Cluster Teams:** Top teams form zonal cluster teams
3. **Sub-Union Day:** Eastern Province Sub-Union Day serves as 3rd round of provincial trials
4. **Playoffs:** Semi-finals and finals determine ultimate winners
5. **National Qualification:** Winners qualify for Standard Bank Mali and FNB Classic Classes Tournament 2026
